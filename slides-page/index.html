ABOUT ME

Videogames are my inspiration.

Even when I was just a kid, I had a passion for gaming and an affinity for my home computer. I remember playing Halo 2, cruising down a bridge in a tank whilst being pursued by a handful of banshees and being asked by my parents what I wanted to do with my life when I grew older. I replied verbatim "I don't know. But it'll definitely be a job involving computers." This vague response grew alongside me throughout middle and high school, particularly with my participation in the instruction of a Programming in Java Computer Science course, and when time came for me to enter university, I was certain that I wanted to pursue a degree in Computer Science.
